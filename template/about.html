About Us

We care for you..!

Located in the vibrant heart of India, Amrita Hospitals is one of the largest private multi-specialist hospitals in Asia. Mata Amritanandamayi Devi laid the hospital's cornerstone in Kochi in 1998. We are dedicated to providing the most sophisticated healthcare to people from all walks of life.

Our Vision

Amrita Hospitals envisions enhancing the health of local communities by providing excellent primary care, specialty care, medical education, and research. As healthcare providers, we consider it our moral obligation to treat every patient with kindness and respect, regardless of their race, socioeconomic status, or belief system.
